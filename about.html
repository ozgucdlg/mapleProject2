ABOUT US

Maple Precast LTD specializes in producing architectural and structural concrete products for residential, Landscape and commercial uses. Customer satisfaction is our goal and with this in mind we work directly with the homeowner or architect in the initial planning and designing phase in order to design the product to meet their specific needs. We help our customers with our expertise to create a custom design home. Kewal Sahota, founder of Maple Precast Inc. has been in the industry for more that 40 years. As head carpenter he is involved in every stage of manufacturing our quality products.
